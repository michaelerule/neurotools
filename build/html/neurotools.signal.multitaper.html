neurotools.signal.multitaper.dpss_cached(length, half_bandwidth_parameter)[source]¶

Get a collection of DPSS tapers. N-tapers = half_bandwidth_parameter*2

For legacy reasons this also transposes the tapes returned, such that the first dimension indexes tapers rather than time.
